AR-ECDIS: A Mobile Maritime Navigation Assistance System

Cagatay Undeger --SimBT INC.

Session Description

"An Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS) is a geographic information system used for nautical navigation . ECDIS provides continuous position and navigational safety information. The system generates audible and/or visual alarms when the vessel is in proximity to navigational hazards. In this presentation, we will discuss the current use of console based ECDIS and explain how the smart-glass integrated ECDIS could liberate the ship’s Officer of the Watch (OOW), providing panoramic 360-degree situational awareness. With AR-ECDIS, sailors can have ship’s route, course, speed, radar, guard zone, and many other vital information on their heads-up display (HUD), in the form of convenient augmented reality (AR) glass. The system provides sailors to see 3D view of the terrain as well. Another major benefit of an AR integrated ECDIS is enhanced command and control during night time or periods of poor visibility, enabling the OOW to maintain heads up awareness digitally augmented/overlaid to the real sea and terrain surface, while also providing data for points of interest not visible with the naked eye. Finally, OOWs can become more effective in decision making by visualizing the relevant data, messages, and warning fused on the HUD."

Bio
