Building an Immersive Platform for Professional & Family Caregivers

Erin Washington --Embodied Labs

Session Description

Attendees of this session will learn about how Embodied Labs built a business around an XR training platform in the aging care space. After attending, participants will be able to identify: 1) the top challenges and potential solutions when building a business in the XR training space; 2) characteristics of and use cases for embodied learning solutions; and 3) the ways Embodied Labs has used data to drive value for its organization.

Bio

Erin Washington is a co-founder and Chief Product Officer of Embodied Labs. Her experience in information science, instructional design with emerging technologies including VR/XR, and as a family caregiver has helped the company develop an immersive platform for professional and family care partners of older adults over the past four years.`
