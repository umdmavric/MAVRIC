Abstract to Action, Adaptive Flight Trainer and Targeted Learning System

Travis H. Sheets --USAF/Pilot

Session Description

This research study was conducted in a 7 month time frame and takes a practitioner’s approach to transform abstract ideas into actionable options for the future of learning. The TLST is an immersive, student-centered, multi-modal learning structure that empowers the learner and leverages emerging technology to provide high fidelity assessments and feedback. Pioneer—an approach to learning that combines the imagination of humans with the intelligence of machines Provide—individuals & organizations more educational & training value than traditional methods/structures Produce—aviation knowledge & skills using immersive environments, performance measurements & adaptive feedback to maximize learning through a student-centered, mutli-modal framework

Bio

Maj. Travis H. Sheets is a USAF pilot, assigned to the Joint Staff. He is currently detailed to OSD Defense Innovation Board, serving as an Innovation Fellow.
